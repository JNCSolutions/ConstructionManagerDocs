--- layout: default title: Quotes ---

Create, modify and manage your quotes.

The first thing you see when you enter the quotes screen is your list of current quotes. The list shows you basic information about each quote, and from the list you can pick quotes to modify, remove, or mail.

On the quote screen itself, you can change the customer, status, date and notes associated with a quote. You can also attach items (both text items with no associated stock code, and existing stock items that can be imported via spreadsheet or the desktop version of Construction Manager.

When a quote is ready for invoicing, it can be attached to a new or existing contract and invoiced directly. Once a quote has been invoiced, it can no longer be re-assigned to a different contract.
