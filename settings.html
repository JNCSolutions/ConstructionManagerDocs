Account Settings

You can change your password by entering your current password, new password, and your new password once more to confirm.

It is important that your password is unique, and complex enough to prevent guessing. Google has some good advice on picking a good password.

Email Templates

Construction Manager makes it easy to send reports and invoices, individually or in bulk, to a large number of your clients and customers. Because it's important that your emails are properly presented, you can supply a set of templates to be used by the bulk mailer.

To access the mail settings, click on the cog icon at the top right of Construction Manager, and select `Email Templates`.

Simply enter your message body, default `to` and `cc` fields, and Construction Manager will handle the rest when it is time to send your mails.

To, CC and BCC

As well as `to` addresses, you can also specify addresses for `cc` or `bcc`. The `to` addresses can be selected from the recipient company's registered email addresses. The `cc` and `bcc` addresses can be filled in by hand.

Placeholders

You can add a personal touch to your email templates with our placeholders. If you want a value from your customer records to appear in your mail body, simply add one of these values to your template where you want it to appear:

%COMPANY_NAME%       The name of the recipient company
%CONTACT_NAME%       The name of the human listed as the company's main contact

For example:

Dear %CONTACT_NAME%,

Attached to this email is the invoice for our recently completed work.

Kind regards,
Joe Bloggs
Bloggs Construction Ltd.
