Company Settings

The Company Settings tab allows you to manage company wide settings. You can use this tab to invite new users, manage the access rights of existing users and manage your outgoing mail address.

If you would like outgoing mails from your account to come from a shared address, such as "enquiries@yourcompany.com", you must ask a member of the support team to enable this setting on your account. You will then be able to specify the email address from which all outgoing mail on your account will be set.

User Settings

Changing Your Password

You can change your password by entering your current password, new password, and your new password once more to confirm.

It is important that your password is unique, and complex enough to prevent guessing. Google has some good advice on picking a good password.

Custom Visibility Settings

If you have changed any column visiblity settings on ConstructionManager.net, you can restore the default settings here.

Notifications

You can opt in to receiving different types of notifications from ConstructionManager.net. These notifications will appear in your menu bar. As well as opting in to receive a notification type, you can also set the priority that the notification will appear as.

Defaults

The Defaults are general settings for ConstructionManager.net behaviour.

Quote Defaults

Use quote number as contract number

If this option is selected, ConstructionManager.net will attempt to use the quote number as the job number when creating a new contract from a quote.

Start number

New quote numbers will begin counting upwards from this value.

Default tender template

Tender items from this template will be available for use with your quote items.

Quote Statuses

These statuses will be available on your quotes. You can set a default value for new quotes and you can select which statuses will not be available for selection.
