General Settings

Display Name

This is the name that will appear in the outgoing mails from your account.

Signature

You can enter some text for your email signature. This field supports basic HTML and image placeholders. Image placeholders are explained in the images section.

Images

You can upload images to be used in your emails. These images can be used in your signature or mail bodies. Once you have uploaded an image, you will see a placeholder field. It will look like %%IMAGE:some_file.jpg%%. Simply copy this text into your email signature or body templates, and the image will be inserted into your emails.

Templates

To, CC and BCC

You can add default `cc` and `bcc` addresses for your different types of outgoing mail.

Placeholders

You can add a personal touch to your email templates with our placeholders. If you want a value from your customer records to appear in your mail body, simply add one of these values to your template where you want it to appear:

%COMPANY_NAME%       The name of the recipient company
%CONTACT_NAME%       The name of the human listed as the company's main contact

For example:

Dear %CONTACT_NAME%,

Attached to this email is the invoice for our recently completed work.

Kind regards,
Joe Bloggs
Bloggs Construction Ltd.
